Thai Vegetable Noodle Soup

Description

This wonderful soup takes one pot and 25 minutes. It has plenty of veggies and bright Thai spices."

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced scallions
  • 2 tbsp Thai spice blend
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 4 oz. dry brown rice noodles
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 baby bok choy
  • 1 cup unsweetened, unflavored plant milk
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 4 to 6 fresh basil leaves, torn into pieces

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan combine scallions, Thai Spice Blend, and Â¼ cup water. Cook over medium 2 minutes.
  2. Add stock, green beans, and carrots. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes.
  3. Add noodles, snow peas, broccoli, and bok choy; simmer 5 to 7 minutes more or until noodles are cooked.
  4. Add milk; cook 1 minute. Stir in lime juice and salt. Garnish with basil.
